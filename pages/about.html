Olivia's Story

Born and raised in a modest Silicon Valley neighborhood, Olivia Bennett developed an early appreciation for technology. Surrounded by the innovation hub, she envisioned a world where technology not only connected people but also bridged economic gaps. Growing up in a tight-knit community, Olivia witnessed the challenges of financial adversity. Limited access to technological resources widened the digital divide, hindering the potential for growth, education, and empowerment within her community.

Fueled by a passion for technology and a desire to make a difference, Olivia set out to find a solution. In 2010, facing the mounting electronic waste crisis and inspired by the transformative power of smartphones, she founded Reconditioned Phones.

The journey wasn't without obstacles - from mastering the art of refurbishment to establishing credibility in a competitive industry.Reconditioned Phones has become a beacon of change, offering a curated selection of affordable and reliable smartphones.
Each device embodies Olivia's commitment to accessibility and quality, turning challenges into opportunities for transformative technology.
The company's milestones include gaining prominence in the phone reselling industry and earning accolades for environmental responsibility.
